Bread recipes

These are my current bread recipes. I just have one for dark bread and one for white bread. They're derivatives of two recipes in Water Flour Salt Yeast. Read the book if these don't make any sense to you :^)

Whole wheat blend

Ingredient Quantity Baker's percentage
White flour 400g 60%
Whole wheat flour 175g 27%
Dark rye flour 85g 13%
Water 540g at 90°F 81%
Salt 11g 1.6%
Instant yeast 3/8 teaspoon

Steps:
  1. Autolyse for 30 minutes
  2. Bulk fermentation for 5 hours, with 2 or 3 folds
  3. Proof in fridge for 10 hours

Example schedule: start autolyse at 6:00pm, start bulk fermentation at 6:30pm, put into proofing basket at 11:30pm, bake at 9:30am the next morning

Poolish

Ingredient Quantity Baker's percentage
White flour 300g

Instant yeast 1/16 teaspoon

Final Dough

Ingredient Quantity Baker's percentage
Poolish 600g
White flour 300g 50%
Water 150g at 105° 25%
Salt 13g 2.1%
Instant yeast 1/2 teaspoon

Steps:
  1. Poolish fermentation for 12-14 hours
  2. Bulk fermentation for 3 hours, with 3 or 4 folds
  3. Proof in fridge for 6-7 hours

Example schedule: mix poolish at 8:00am, start bulk fermentation at 9:00pm, put into proofing basket at 12:00am, bake at 7:00am the next morning
